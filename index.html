Hometown Expo

The Hometown Living Expo is a one of a kind Quad Cities Community event that attracted visitors over a 2 day weekend at the QCCA Expo Center. It was comprised of many different types of local businesses and vendors.

The lifestyle show was packed with local expert exhibitors showcasing hundreds of products, ideas, and services for all area of life including home remodeling, beauty, wellness, pet care, real estate, financial planning, outdoor spaces, culinary experiences, boutiques, and much more. A wonderful range of exhibitors with ideas and inspiration to improve your home & lifestyle in one location. I worked with Julie to help bring her online identity to life and make information accessible to all those that joined in on this one of a kind event.

Color Lab

The Color Lab is an Open Source web application that facilitates Color theory principles, helping designers make informed decisions about color harmony, contrast, as well as balance. It is a tool that helps to improve worlflow efficiency and enhances creativity among multiple mediums. The tool was created to facilitate consistency across multiple projects and ensuring that colors match the intended design and brand guidelines.

Deb's Daily Grind

Deb's Daily Grind is an eCommerce store that we built for Deb as she wanted to transition from her mobile coffee business to selling her products online. Deb's Daily Grind is now an online storefront selling specialty fresh roasted coffee beans as well as loose leaf teas and a line up of other options.

Deb chose to use Shopify as the platform to get her up and running. We helped ensure a seamless and effective online storefront by listening to her requirements and learned about her target audience to tailor the site specifically to her needs. The site was carefully planned for optimal functioning for devices of all sizes, and boasts quick loading times as well as search engine visibility.

Cryptosis

A growing news source with the latest updates, and analysis in decentralized finance. This project began as a twitter community that shares impactful stories that shape the ecosystem. The owner of the group decided they wanted to expand their operations into other communities and wanted the backbone of the expansion to be new branding and an interactive website.

We were approached to guide in the branding and conversion of the articles into a website that allowed their small community to share headlines and grow the brand into something larger. The website displays our talent in creating interactive websites using animations and showcases the latest trends in digital design. Logo design, branding, web design, and the development of the website was entirely in our hands.
